About Ilesh Cereal Shop

Located in Kasarani, Nairobi, Ilesh Cereal Shop offers a variety of high-quality cereals, including maize, beans, green grams, rice, and more. We are committed to quality, affordability, and excellent customer service.

Our Products

Maize & Flour

Freshly milled maize flour and dry maize available in bulk or retail.

Beans & Legumes

We stock beans, green grams, ndengu, and lentils — clean and ready to cook.

Rice & Grains

Affordable long grain rice and other cereals sourced from trusted suppliers.

Accessibility Commitment

We are dedicated to serving every customer. Our shop is accessible to all and we strive to provide clear product labeling and assistance when needed. This website is also designed to be accessible for screen readers and mobile users.
